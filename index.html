Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/16/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Eroot Labs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our websites and mobile applications published on the Google Play Store.

This policy is designed to comply with Google Play policies, COPPA, CalOPPA, and applicable privacy regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Contacts
  • Photos, videos, or media files

If our website or app requires optional user interaction (such as contact forms or newsletter subscriptions), users may voluntarily provide limited information like an email address.

2. When We Collect Information

Information is collected only when users voluntarily submit it through our website or use app features that require it.

3. How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Improve user experience
  • Improve website or app functionality
  • Respond to user inquiries
  • Analyze usage trends (non-personal data)

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. Cookies help us:

  • Understand user preferences
  • Compile aggregate usage statistics

You can disable cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Our apps and website may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Analytics
  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (analytics and crash reporting)

These services may collect anonymous usage data under their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Advertising

We may display ads via Google AdMob or other Google-certified ad providers.

  • Ads may be non-personalized
  • Kids apps follow Google Families Ad policies
  • No interest-based ads for children

7. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Apps designed for children:

  • Do not collect personal information
  • Do not include social sharing
  • Show only child-appropriate ads (if any)

Parents may contact us if they believe data has been collected unintentionally.

8. California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA)

In accordance with CalOPPA:

  • Users may visit our site anonymously
  • This privacy policy is clearly accessible
  • Changes will be posted on this page

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.
